Amsterdam Cookies

Early in the morning, you work on a code, your eyes start to get tired... what will help you the most now? Amsterdam cookies!
Delicious, rich in chocolate and most importantly - soft and surprising.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp baking
  • 1 tsp vanilla powder
  • A pinch salt
  • 1/2 bag of baking powder
  • 120g butter
  • 1 egg
  • Chocolate chips
  • Filling: You can use white / brown chocolate coins, kinder chocolate cubes and anything else that comes to mind.

How to

  1. Transfer to the mixer bowl all the ingredients except the chocolate chips.
  2. Mix with a guitar hook until a uniform dough is obtained.
  3. Add the chocolate chips and mix again.
  4. Cut small circles and flatten on the palm.
  5. Place the filling we chose in the center.
  6. Wrap the filling with the dough into a ball shape and place on a baking sheet.
  7. Place the balls at intervals of about 4 cm.
  8. Place in a preheated oven at 175 degrees.
  9. Bake for exactly 8 minutes !!!!

Bon Appetit!
